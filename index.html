Ziheng Ji 季梓衡

M.S. Student · Shanghai Jiao Tong University · Embodied AI / VLA / Robot Simulation

I am a Master's student at the Global Institute of Future Technology, Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU), advised by Prof. Tong Qin. I previously earned my B.E. in Vehicle Engineering from Beijing Institute of Technology (BIT). My research focuses on embodied AI, vision-language-action (VLA) models, and real-to-sim robot simulation — building high-fidelity digital twins (3DGS) and Sim2Real policy-training pipelines. I am currently an algorithm engineer at Xiaomi Embodied Intelligence.

Research Interests

Embodied AI & VLA

Vision-language-action policies for long-horizon, contact-rich manipulation on real robots.

Robot Simulation & Digital Twins

Real-to-sim pipelines with Isaac Sim, MuJoCo and 3DGS rendering for high-fidelity training.

Sim2Real Transfer

Imitation & reinforcement learning that crosses the reality gap to physical hardware.

SLAM & End-to-End Driving

Localization, 3D reconstruction and end-to-end perception-to-planning systems.
